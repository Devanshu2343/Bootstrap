AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the ability of machines to imitate human intelligence, enabling them to learn, plan, think, and solve problems. It involves developing computer systems capable of performing tasks that traditionally require human intelligence, such as reasoning, decision-making, and problem-solving. AI encompasses technologies like machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing.
